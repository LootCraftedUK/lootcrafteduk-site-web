About Us

LootCraftedUK is an online store born out of a passion for gaming, cats and creativity. We design and produce unique print‑on‑demand merchandise that lets you show off your personality. Each piece is made to order and shipped from the UK, reducing waste and ensuring your items are fresh off the press.

From quirky t‑shirts to stylish mugs and vibrant wall art, our products are crafted with care and love. Whether you're grinding XP or curled up with your cats, we've got something to make your day brighter.
